Signup Procedure

Following are the steps in registering the entity on NGO DARPAN:

Sign Up for New NGOs:

  • Provide your NGO Name
  • Provide Email (OTP is sent for confirmation)
  • Provide Mobile Number (OTP confirmation required)
  • Enter security code displayed on the website
  • Confirm OTPs
  • Enter NGO PAN Number
  • After verification, create a password

Login Procedure for Registered NGOs:

  • Click on Sign In link
  • Provide PAN Number, Mobile, or Email
  • Enter your password
  • Access the Dashboard
  • Forgot password? Use OTP to reset
